## UNITED NATIONS SPACE COMMAND

The United Nations Space Command maintains primary military authority in the subsector under terms established by the Kestrel Accord.

Operational focus includes:

- protection of trade corridors
- frontier defense
- anti-piracy operations
- Banished containment
- anomalous region interdiction

Anchorpoint serves as primary command center.

Principal naval deterrence remains the Marathon-class heavy cruiser  
UNSC If They Came To Hear Me Beg

```
READINESS..................OPERATIONAL
FLEET STRENGTH.............LIMITED
STRATEGIC POSTURE..........DEFENSIVE
```

---

## HIGHMARK COLONIAL AUTHORITY

Civil governance of Highmark remains under colonial administration, though military influence has expanded considerably since the war.

Political priorities include:

- colonial autonomy
- frontier security
- economic recovery
- management of offworld influence

Relations with UNSC command remain cooperative, though strained.

Local sentiment toward central authority remains mixed.

---

## OUTPOST VORUN

Joint diplomatic and military presence maintained by the Swords of Sanghelios.

Mission includes:

- alliance coordination
- joint patrol operations
- anti-Banished response
- strategic advisory presence

Though politically sensitive, Vorun remains a stabilizing force within the subsector.

```
ALLIANCE STATUS............STABLE
JOINT OPERATIONS...........ONGOING
PUBLIC ACCEPTANCE..........VARIABLE
```

## BANISHED WARBAND PRESENCE

Localized The Banished warband activity persists throughout frontier systems.

Primary patterns include:

- convoy raids
- salvage interdiction
- relic-seeking operations
- peripheral settlement strikes

Threat scale remains below occupation-level, but sufficient to destabilize outlying systems.

Interest in Calder remains suspected.

```
HOSTILE ACTIVITY...........CONFIRMED
RAID FREQUENCY.............INTERMITTENT
ESCALATION RISK............MODERATE
```
