# Halo: Kestrel Player Glossary

> [!info] PLAYER REFERENCE  
> This glossary is intended for players unfamiliar with the Halo universe or its terminology. These definitions are simplified for ease of understanding and represent common knowledge within the setting.

---

# A

## AI

Artificial intelligences used extensively by the UNSC. There are two major categories:

- Dumb AI: Specialized systems designed for specific tasks.
    
- Smart AI: Highly advanced AIs capable of learning, adaptation, and independent thought.
    

## Ancilla

An ancient Forerunner term for artificial intelligences.

## Arbiter

A title historically given to a Sangheili warrior chosen to carry out dangerous or politically sensitive missions. In the modern era, the Arbiter is a major leader within the Swords of Sanghelios.

---

# B

## Banished

A powerful mercenary empire and splinter faction formed from former Covenant species. Known for brutality, scavenged equipment, and aggressive expansion.

## Battle Dress

Powered combat armor used in advanced military operations. In Halo terms, MJOLNIR armor is considered a form of battle dress.

## BR / Battle Rifle

A UNSC burst-fire rifle designed for precision combat.

---

# C

## Covenant

A former alliance of alien species united under a religious hierarchy. The Covenant waged war against humanity for nearly three decades before collapsing.

## Covenant War / Human-Covenant War

The war fought between humanity and the Covenant from 2525 to 2552. Humanity suffered catastrophic losses before eventually surviving the conflict.

## Cortana

One of the most famous Smart AIs ever created by humanity. Closely associated with the Spartan known as Master Chief.

## Cryo / Cryosleep

Suspended animation used for long-duration space travel.

---

# D

## DMR

Designated Marksman Rifle. A precision rifle commonly used by UNSC personnel.

## Drop Pod

A heavily armored insertion vehicle used by ODSTs and other UNSC forces to deploy from orbit directly into combat zones.

---

# E

## Energy Shield

A rechargeable defensive energy barrier commonly used by Covenant species and Spartan MJOLNIR armor.

---

# F

## FleetCom

UNSC Fleet Command. Oversees naval operations.

## Flood

A highly dangerous parasitic lifeform capable of infecting and consuming sentient species. Considered one of the greatest threats in known space.

## Forerunners

An ancient and extremely advanced civilization that existed long before humanity. Responsible for constructing the Halo Array and numerous megastructures.

## Frigate

A common class of UNSC warship used for escort, patrol, and frontline combat.

---

# G

## Glassing

The process of bombarding a planet from orbit with plasma weaponry until the surface is vitrified into glass.

## Grav Lift

A Covenant or Banished anti-gravity field used for transportation.

---

# I

## Insurrection / Insurrectionists

Anti-UEG and anti-UNSC rebel movements primarily originating from the Outer Colonies.

## Inner Colonies

Human worlds located closer to Earth and heavily tied to the UEG.

---

# H

## Halo Ring

Massive ring-shaped superweapons created by the Forerunners.

## Headhunters

Specialized Spartan-III operatives assigned to covert assassination and infiltration missions.

## HUD

Heads-Up Display. A helmet-based interface displaying targeting, communications, and tactical information.

## Huragok

Also known as Engineers. Floating biological organisms created by the Forerunners to maintain and repair technology. Generally non-combatants and highly valued for their technical abilities.

---

# I

## Jump / Slipspace Jump

Travel through Slipspace to move faster than light between star systems.

---

# K

## Kig-Yar

Also known as Jackals. Agile alien species known for piracy, scouting, and marksmanship.

---

# L

## Lekgolo

Worm-like colonial organisms that operate collectively in large groups. Lekgolo colonies are capable of inhabiting armor, machinery, and other structures.

---

# M

## MAC Cannon

Magnetic Accelerator Cannon. A powerful electromagnetic weapon system used by the UNSC on ships and orbital defense platforms.

## Marine

Standard infantry branch of the UNSC military.

## Master Chief

The most famous Spartan in human history. Public hero of the Human-Covenant War.

## MJOLNIR

Advanced powered assault armor used exclusively by Spartans.

## Mgalekgolo

Also known as Hunters. Massive armored combat forms created when large numbers of Lekgolo organisms bond together inside specialized armor.

---

# O

## ODST

Orbital Drop Shock Trooper. Elite UNSC shock infantry deployed from orbit using drop pods.

## ONI

Office of Naval Intelligence. The UNSC's intelligence and black operations agency.

## Outer Colonies

Frontier human worlds located far from Earth. Often more independent and distrustful of centralized authority.

---

# P

## Plasma Weaponry

Energy-based weapons primarily used by Covenant species.

## Prometheans

Forerunner combat constructs controlled by advanced artificial intelligence systems.

---

# S

## Sangheili

Also known as Elites. Highly martial alien species formerly central to the Covenant.

## Section III

A division within ONI responsible for military projects, intelligence operations, and classified programs.

## Smart AI

Highly advanced artificial intelligences capable of independent thought and personality development.

## Spartan

Members of a series of UNSC supersoldier programs designed to create highly augmented elite soldiers.

## Spartan-II

The second generation Spartan program. Extremely effective but controversial due to the use of child candidates.

## Spartan-III

A later Spartan generation designed for large-scale special operations.

## Spartan-IV

The modern Spartan generation composed primarily of adult military volunteers.

## Spartan Operations

The UNSC branch overseeing Spartan personnel after the Human-Covenant War.

## SPI Armor

Semi-Powered Infiltration armor. Stealth-focused armor system often associated with Spartan-III operatives.

## Slipspace

An alternate dimension used for faster-than-light travel.

## Slipspace Drive

The engine system allowing spacecraft to enter Slipspace.

## Swords of Sanghelios

A Sangheili-led faction allied with humanity following the collapse of the Covenant.

---

# U

## UEG

Unified Earth Government. Humanity's primary civilian governing body.

## UNSC

United Nations Space Command. Humanity's primary military organization.

---

# W

## Warthog

A versatile UNSC military utility vehicle.

## War Games

Simulated combat exercises often used to train Spartans.

---

# X

## Xenoarchaeology

The study of alien ruins and artifacts.
