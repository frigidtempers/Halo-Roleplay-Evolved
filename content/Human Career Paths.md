Spartan Career Paths:
	Spartan IV:
		Create a Human character as normal, Spartan career path can be rolled for entry as a Life Event rolled on the Life Events table for other careers and Pre Career Education. Following admittance into this Career Path players are given Spartan IV Augmentation Package as well as Mjolnir Armor (Gen 2)
	If players wish to play earlier generation Spartans they must roll prior to taking any kind of pre-career education or career path. On a 12 they are selected for the Spartan II or III program and begin Spartan Character Creation process.
Spartan Character Creation:
	Start as Human, Pre-Career Education use Spartan Training Pre-Career Education path. Players graduate at age 14 and begin Spartan Career Path at that age. Take appropriate amount of terms to catch character up age wise to current setting of 2560.
		Spartan II: Recruited in 2517 and graduate in 2525, take 9 terms in Spartan Career path to get to 2560. Characters will be around age 49 chronologically (note that cryosleep factors into biological age so subtract 1d6 years)
		Spartan III: Recruited in 2532 and graduate in 2536, take 6 terms in Spartan Career path to get to 2560.  Characters will be around age 36 chronologically (subtract 1d6 years for cryosleep)
	
*Notes for Spartans and Military Personnel*
	Spartans and Military Career Path characters will be subject to military command structures UNLESS specified to be mercenary (Spartans can not be mercenaries.) 

Modifications to existing Career Paths
	Change Army to Marine, Marine path to ODST
	Covenant species will receive their own specialized career paths should players wish to play as one. HOWEVER it should be noted and discussed with players prior to starting the game if anyone is planning on playing a Covenant species so I don’t do any extra work.
