## UNSC ARMORS

> [!info] STANDARD ISSUE EQUIPMENT  
> The following systems are approved for use by UNSC personnel operating within the Kestrel Subsector.
### UNSC Battle Dress Uniform (BDU)

> [!note] LINE INFANTRY ARMOR  
> Standard-issue combat uniform for UNSC Marines. Designed for durability, flexibility, and ease of deployment across varied environments.

```
Use Lightweight Poly Carapace (TL12)
    Add:
        Body Camera (TL10)
        Friend or Foe HUD (TL11)
        Helmet Lights (TL8)
        Eye Protection (TL6)
        Computer Weave (TL11)

    Optional:
        Camouflage, Basic (TL3)
            Note: Represents standard uniform patterning; provides no significant bonus without appropriate terrain
```
### ODST Battle Dress Uniform

> [!note] SHOCK TROOPER ARMOR

## UNSC BALLISTIC WEAPONS

> [!info] APPROVED SMALL ARMS  
> Standard UNSC ballistic weaponry. Variants may exist depending on supply, manufacturer, and operational theater.
### MA40 Assault Rifle

> [!note] STANDARD SERVICE RIFLE  
> Primary infantry weapon of the UNSC.

```
Use Advanced Combat Rifle
    Reduce Magazine to 36
    Remove Scope
```
### BR75 Battle Rifle

> [!note] PRECISION BURST RIFLE  
> Designed for controlled engagements at medium range.

```
Use Heavy Advanced Combat Rifle
    Reduce Magazine to 36
    Restricted to Burst or Single Fire
    Remove Bulky
```
### M20 SMG

> [!note] PERSONAL DEFENSE WEAPON  
> High rate-of-fire weapon for confined environments.

```
Use Submachine Gun
    Increase Magazine to 60
```
### M395B DMR

> [!note] DESIGNATED MARKSMAN RIFLE  
> Semi-automatic precision rifle.

```
Use Rifle
    Add Scope
    Increase Magazine to 15
    Add AP 2
```
### VK78 Commando Rifle

> [!note] SPECIALIZED ASSAULT PLATFORM  
> Hybrid rifle bridging automatic and precision roles.

```
Use Autorifle
    Add Scope
```
### SR99-S7 Sniper Rifle

> [!note] LONG-RANGE PRECISION RIFLE  
> Anti-material sniper platform.

```
Use Gauss Sniper Rifle
    Note: Treated as standard ballistic weapon; use stats only, ignore Gauss traits

> [!note] HEAVY SIDEARM

### CQS48 Bulldog
