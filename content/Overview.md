# SECTION III // OUTER COLONY SURVEY

**FILE:** Kestrel Subsector Overview  
**CLEARANCE:** Restricted  
**SOURCE:** UNSC NAVCOM / ONI Regional Briefing

_The Kestrel Subsector occupies a sparsely settled frontier region at the edge of former UNSC Outer Colony territory. Long considered strategically marginal, the region has assumed renewed importance in 2560 due to instability across human space and increased activity along frontier trade corridors._

_The subsector remains lightly populated and unevenly defended. Civil authority, naval command, colonial interests, and allied Sangheili presence operate under the framework established by the Kestrel Accord, though tensions remain._

Primary regional anchor is the colony world **Kestrel**, known locally as **Highmark**.

Common sentiment recorded among local populations:

> “They weren’t there then. They’re here now.”

Interpretation: resentment present, insurgent activity minimal.

**Highmark (Kestrel)**  
Outer Colony frontier world. Regional administrative and military center. Site of Anchorpoint and Outpost Vorun.

**Meridian**  
Industrial support world. Shipyard and logistics center.

**Caedmon**  
Older agricultural colony with strong traditionalist identity.

**Vigilance**  
Forward defense system and allied security bastion.

**Drift**  
Remote system with limited survey data and persistent anomalous reports.

**Port Lowell**  
Peripheral trade port with elevated smuggling and piracy activity.

**Calder**  
Glassed colony world under restricted access. ONI oversight active.

**K-081/B**  
Restricted anomalous region. Navigation access controlled.

Further inquiries require higher clearance.

## STRATEGIC CONDITIONS

Regional security remains stable but strained.

```
THREAT VECTOR 01........Banished Raiding Activity
THREAT VECTOR 02........Colonial Political Friction
THREAT VECTOR 03........Calder / K-081/B Anomalies
```

Threat conditions include:

- Banished raids in frontier systems
- Smuggling and armed traffic near Port Lowell
- Political friction tied to military expansion on Highmark
- Unresolved anomalies associated with Calder and K-081/B

No major Banished occupation presence identified.

Localized warband activity remains sufficient to threaten shipping and outlying settlements.

Personnel are advised not to interfere.
