Use Mongoose Traveller but modify it for use with Halo Concepts

Things to consider: Power Armor, Species Traits, Slipspace travel vs Jump Drives, Cryogenics, Forerunner tech

Species:
Humans (Self Explanatory)
	No stat buffs directly, “Traveler Default”
Sangheili (Elites)
	+1 Str and Int, For Pre-Career Education only Military Academy may be selected. 
Jiralhanae (Brutes)
	+2 Str and End, -2 Int and Edu, Limited to Military and Military adjacent career paths.
Unggoy (Grunts)
	+2 End -2 Soc, breathe methane instead of oxygen so can not breathe freely without special equipment or a methane rich atmosphere
Jackals
	+2 Dex +1 End, -2 Str -1 Int. Limited to Rogue and Mercenary career paths

Non-Playable Species:
- Drones
- Hunters
- Smart AIs
